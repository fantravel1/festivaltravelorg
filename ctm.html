CTM Festival

Berlin, Germany

Overview

CTM is a week‑long festival in Berlin that celebrates adventurous music and related visual arts. Since its inception in 1999 it has continuously evolved and is now recognised for its inclusive and socially engaged approach.

  • One of the safest and most female‑friendly festivals in the world, operating under the banner “Festival for Adventurous Music and Related Visual Arts”.
  • Supports local and international arts, media cultures and technological developments, while addressing social issues through music and art.
  • Features a world‑class line‑up of musicians, artists and thinkers who push boundaries across genres.

Why It’s Great for Women

CTM’s commitment to diversity and social responsibility creates a welcoming space for women and other under‑represented groups. Its programming encourages critical conversations alongside unforgettable performances.
