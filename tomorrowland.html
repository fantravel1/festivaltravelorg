Tomorrowland

Boom, Belgium

Overview

Tomorrowland is one of the world’s most iconic music festivals, renowned for its elaborate stages, immersive decor and global community. Held in Boom, Belgium, it has been captivating audiences since 2005.

  • Extends over two weekends and tickets sell out within minutes.
  • Celebrated for its vibrant atmosphere and immersive experience.
  • Notable for being safe, friendly and welcoming for women and LGBTQ+ participants.
  • Offers an impressive international line‑up and spectacular stage design, ensuring a once‑in‑a‑lifetime experience.

Why It’s Great for Women

Tomorrowland’s reputation for safety and inclusivity, combined with its magical setting and outstanding production, makes it a dream destination for female festival goers. The community spirit on site means you’ll always find a friendly face.
