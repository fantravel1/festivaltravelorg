Safe Hotels & Accommodation

Tips and curated stays for solo female travellers and groups

How to Choose a Female‑Friendly Hotel

Finding the right place to rest is just as important as the festival itself. These guidelines, inspired by expert advice, will help you pick accommodation that’s safe and comfortable.

  • Rely on personal communities and trusted reviews rather than marketing claims when selecting hotels.
  • Look for essential safety features such as 24×7 manned reception, double‑locking doors, a good location with a well‑lit approach and spy holes.
  • Seek out comfort touches like full‑length mirrors, plug sockets near mirrors, decent hair‑dryers, removable coat‑hangers and luxury toiletries.
  • Some properties offer ladies‑only floors, such as the Pearl Floor at the Four Seasons Riyadh, which provides extra privacy and security.
