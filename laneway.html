Laneway Festival

Australia & Singapore

Overview

Laneway Festival started in Melbourne and has grown into a multi‑city event hosted across Australia, New Zealand and Singapore. It is widely regarded as one of the most female‑friendly festivals on the circuit.

  • More than half of the performers are female and the line‑up spans all musical genres.
  • Unearthed artists are welcomed regardless of gender, providing equal opportunities for emerging talent.
  • Held simultaneously in cities such as Melbourne, Adelaide, Auckland, Brisbane, Sydney, Fremantle and Singapore.
  • Proceeds support causes like Plus 1, the Dean Turner Project and Fight NMD.

Why It’s Great for Women

With a diverse and inclusive line‑up and a strong commitment to social causes, Laneway creates a safe space for women to enjoy music. The festival’s multiple locations also make it easier to plan a trip that suits your travel itinerary.
