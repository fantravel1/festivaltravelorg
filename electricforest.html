Electric Forest

Rothbury, Michigan, USA

Overview

Electric Forest is a four‑day festival in Rothbury, Michigan, celebrated for its immersive atmosphere and commitment to creating safe spaces for women.

  • Her Forest programme offers a supportive and empowering environment for women attending and camping alone.
  • The programme features a women’s group camp located in the heart of general camping where female forest‑goers can meet and share their experiences.
  • An eclectic line‑up of artists and a magical woodland setting make Electric Forest a unique and inclusive celebration.

Why It’s Great for Women

With designated group camping and programmes designed specifically for women, Electric Forest fosters camaraderie and safety. Its welcoming atmosphere and supportive community mean solo travellers can enjoy the festival with confidence.
