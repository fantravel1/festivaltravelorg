Safe Nightlife & Party Guide

Stay empowered on the dancefloor with safety tips and inclusive events

Nightlife Safety Tips

Heading out for a night of music and dancing? Keep these tips in mind to ensure your evening is both fun and safe.

  • Share your location with trusted friends and check in periodically so others know you’re safe.
  • Keep your drink in sight at all times and never accept beverages from strangers.
  • Pace yourself by drinking water between alcoholic drinks and stay mindful of your limits.
  • Be a good bystander – step in or seek help if you notice someone in an uncomfortable situation.
  • Carry fentanyl test strips and naloxone if available, and practise safe sex.
  • Keep your phone charged and verify rideshare vehicles before getting in; avoid sending drunk friends off alone.
  • Plan your night with a group, stay aware of your surroundings and trust your intuition.

Inclusive Party Spots

Looking for nightlife that welcomes women and gender‑diverse people? These parties and clubs embrace inclusivity and community.

Papi Juice

Location: New York City, USA

A monthly party for queer and trans people of colour where merengue, voguing anthems and R&B fill the dancefloor.

Minh

Location: Hong Kong

A non‑flashy club offering an alternative nightlife space for audiophiles and dancefloor introverts.

Same‑Sex Saturdays

Location: Durban, South Africa

A long‑running night for black South African LGBTQI+ clubbers and DJs, fostering community and celebration.

Cool Room

Location: Melbourne, Australia

A house and techno rave that strives for diversity across gender, race, sexual identity and physical ability.

What Makes a Safe Space Party?

Promoters and activists around the world are working to create safer space parties where everyone can feel free and respected. These events prioritise clear communication of expectations, boundaries and consequences. Organisers centre marginalised voices and advocate for consent, respect and harm reduction, turning dancefloors into vibrant, fun and transformative spaces for all.
