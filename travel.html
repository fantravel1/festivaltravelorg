Safe Destinations for Solo Female Travellers

These destinations stand out for their safety records, welcoming locals and memorable experiences. Each summary comes from first‑hand accounts of solo female travellers.

Maun, Botswana

Maun is the gateway to the Okavango Delta and a safe base for exploring Botswana.

  • Low crime rates and a relaxed atmosphere make it a great base for exploring.
  • Warm and friendly locals help visitors feel welcome.
  • Perfect starting point for camping trips into the Okavango Delta and wildlife spotting.

Abu Dhabi, UAE

A modern city with a low crime rate and respectful culture.

  • One of the safest cities in the world for solo female travel.
  • Clean, modern and respectful culture where it’s easy to feel comfortable walking alone.
  • Beautiful beaches such as Corniche Beach and a wealth of attractions make for a balanced trip.

Iceland

A peaceful country renowned for its natural wonders and welcoming people.

  • Boasts one of the lowest crime rates on the planet and friendly, welcoming locals.
  • Ideal for adventure lovers thanks to volcanos, glaciers, waterfalls and hot springs.
  • Reykjavik is compact and easy to navigate, offering a relaxed city experience.

Thailand

A vibrant country with friendly people and low crime against tourists.

  • Warm, friendly locals and low crime rates help travellers feel safe.
  • Easy to meet fellow travellers along the backpacker trail.
  • Stories of kindness abound – locals go out of their way to help visitors in need.
  • Recommended stay: Pho Place in Bangkok – an inexpensive, clean and quiet hotel tucked away in busy Chinatown.

Paris, France

The city of lights is perfect for romantic solo adventures.

  • Paris is a wonderful city for first‑time solo travellers – its charm makes it easy to fall in love.
  • Wander beautiful streets, iconic sights and cafés at your own pace.
  • Favourite hotel: Grand Hôtel Lévêque on Rue Cler, which boasts a charming location near the Eiffel Tower.

Corfu, Greece

An island paradise with friendly locals and low crime.

  • Corfu has some of the lowest crime rates in Greece and Europe.
  • The Greek people are outgoing and welcoming, helping visitors feel safe.
  • Recommended area: Paleokastritsa with stunning beaches, plenty of restaurants and many accommodation options.

Finding Female‑Friendly Hotels

Choosing the right accommodation is key to feeling safe and comfortable on your travels. Here are our top tips for finding female‑friendly hotels:

  • Rely on personal communities, trusted influencers, friends and independent reviews – they’re often more reliable than marketing.
  • Look for essential safety features such as a 24×7 manned reception, double‑locking doors, a well‑lit approach and a good neighbourhood.
  • Opt for hotels that offer spy holes and place solo women in secure areas of the building.
  • Comfort matters too: full‑length mirrors, plug sockets near mirrors, decent hair‑dryers, removable coat hangers and luxury bathroom products can make your stay pleasant.
  • Some properties offer women‑only floors – for example, the Four Seasons in Riyadh has a dedicated Pearl Floor for lone female travellers.
