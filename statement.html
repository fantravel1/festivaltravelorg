Statement Festival

Gothenburg, Sweden

Overview

The Statement Festival is a groundbreaking event held in Gothenburg. It was created as a direct response to assaults at other festivals and has become a sanctuary for women and gender‑diverse music fans.

  • Held as the world’s first cisgender male‑free festival, welcoming women, non‑binary and transgender attendees.
  • Founded by comedian Emma Knyckare after sexual assaults at Sweden’s Bråvalla festival to provide a safe place where people could enjoy music without fear.
  • Organisers highlight the festival’s success and emphasise the need for spaces that prioritise safety and respect.

Why It’s Great for Women

By centering women, non‑binary and transgender people, Statement Festival offers a music experience free from harassment. The community spirit and activist roots make it a refuge where female festival‑goers can dance and explore without compromise.
