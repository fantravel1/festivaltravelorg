Igloofest

Montreal, Canada

Overview

Igloofest is a winter festival in Montreal known as the coldest music festival in the world. Despite the chilly temperatures, it provides a warm and inclusive atmosphere where women can feel safe and welcome.

  • Considered a gender‑equal festival where organisers take measures to prevent harassment, invasion of personal space and bullying.
  • Encourages attendees to protect themselves from freezing temperatures by dressing appropriately.
  • Offers all‑in packages that include lodging, tickets and transfers for a hassle‑free experience.
  • Features nine nights of activities and performances from world‑class artists such as DJ Esco, John Digweed, Bonobo and Sasha.

Why It’s Great for Women

Igloofest’s commitment to safety and equality makes it a standout choice for female travellers. Its friendly crowd and dedicated staff create an environment where women can dance, socialise and enjoy music without fear.
