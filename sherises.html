She Rises Festival

Taos, New Mexico, USA

Overview

She Rises Music Festival is set against the stunning backdrop of Taos, New Mexico. More than just a concert, it’s a transformative celebration of women in music that aims to empower artists and uplift communities.

  • Empowers female artists and fosters community connection while supporting Music Helps programmes for traumatised children, hospitalised youngsters and women in shelters.
  • Bridges art and heart by uplifting women in music and creating lasting social impact.
  • Showcases an international all‑female line‑up of musicians, entrepreneurs and creatives, welcoming everyone to celebrate diversity and creativity.

Why It’s Great for Women

She Rises offers a safe, inclusive atmosphere where women can connect with each other, celebrate female talent and contribute to meaningful causes. With its focus on community and charity, the festival invites attendees to dance, learn and be inspired.
