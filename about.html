About Us

Who we are and why we created this library.

Our Mission

FestivalsForWomen was created by music lovers who believe everyone deserves to experience the joy of live music without compromising safety. We curate the best female‑friendly festivals, provide practical travel advice and share safety tips so you can confidently explore the world’s music scenes.

We’re a community‑driven platform. If you’ve attended a festival that prioritises inclusion and safety, or if you have recommendations for safe hotels, parties or destinations, please get in touch and help us grow this library.
