Snowbombing

Mayrhofen, Austria

Overview

Snowbombing is a unique alpine festival that combines music with adventure. Hosted in the picturesque town of Mayrhofen, the event offers an incredible line‑up alongside mountain activities.

  • Winner of the “Best Small Festival” at the 2016 European Festival Awards.
  • Week‑long event featuring world‑famous bands, artists and DJs.
  • Signature activities include paragliding, Igloo raving, cold beer drinking, fancy dress street parties and alpine yoga classes.
  • Set in an idyllic alpine setting, making it safe and fun for female fans.

Why It’s Great for Women

Snowbombing’s combination of wholesome activities and attentive event staff ensures that women can enjoy the festival and all its mountain adventures with peace of mind.
